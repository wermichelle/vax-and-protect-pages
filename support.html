Vax & Protect Support

Vax & Protect helps you organize dog vaccinations, monthly parasite medicine, and local reminders.

Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

Dog profiles, photos, and treatment records are stored on your device and can sync through your private iCloud database when iCloud is available. You can also export a backup file from Settings.

When are parasite treatments due?

When you select Antiparasite, the next due date defaults to one calendar month after the administered date. You can adjust it before saving.

Why did I not receive a reminder?

Open Settings in Vax & Protect and confirm notifications are enabled. Reminders are scheduled for 9:00 AM, 7 days and 1 day before a future due date.

How do I delete my information?

Delete treatment records from a dog’s treatment history. Deleting a dog removes that profile and its associated treatment records. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletions can sync to iCloud. Export a backup file from Settings when you want a separate copy.
