Privacy Policy for Vax & Protect

Effective date: June 9, 2026

Vax & Protect is designed to keep your pet-care information private.

Information You Add

You may add dog names, breeds, gender, ear tag numbers, birthdates, profile photos, weight, vaccination details, antiparasite treatment details, clinic names, notes, and due dates.

How Information Is Stored

This information is stored using Apple’s SwiftData framework. When iCloud sync is enabled, Apple CloudKit stores and syncs the data in your private iCloud database for devices signed in to your Apple ID. Vax & Protect does not send this information to the developer, advertising networks, analytics services, or other third parties.

The app also lets you export a backup file that you control. If you choose to save or share that file, it is handled by Apple’s system file and sharing interfaces.

Photos

The app uses Apple’s system photo picker. A photo is copied into the app only when you explicitly select it.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Vax & Protect schedules local reminders on your device for upcoming treatment due dates and optional monthly backup reminders. Notification scheduling does not send your pet-care information to the developer.

Tracking and Analytics

Vax & Protect does not track you, display advertising, use third-party analytics, or collect diagnostics from the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual treatment records or dog profiles within the app. Deleting a dog also deletes its associated treatment history. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletion may also sync to your private iCloud database. You can keep separate backup files by exporting them from Settings.

Children

Vax & Protect is a general pet-care organization tool and is not directed to children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at the Privacy Policy URL shown on the App Store listing.
